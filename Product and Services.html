What we offer

FFCIA is here for those who have issues of anything cancer related. We offer 7 main services which all are cost free and offered to those who need it most.

The first service we offer is diagnostic services where we provide MRI, CT scans, PET scans, X-rays, and mammography that help detect and monitor cancer.









One of our most used serice is our support serice which benefits patients by providing specialized care to treat cancer pain and also provide therapy and support groups to give consulling to both the patient and the family.









We offer the best treatmeant services with state of the art facilites that help patients to get drug treameants that kill or slow the spred of cancer.








Our preventive service include screening programs,vaccinations and public education to spread awareness and HPV vaccines to prevent cervical cancer from occuring.







In order to fight cancer we conduct research and clinical trial to discover new ways to diagnose,prevent, and treat cancer.







To ensure our patients recieve the best customer service we have a paitient navigation service that deals with finicial counseling for our paitents ,case management and survivorship programs.
